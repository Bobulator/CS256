Tennis

Equipment: Tennis racket, tennis balls, tennis court.
Wheather: Clear days with little or no wind.

The Experience
Tennis is a fast-paced sport that combines reflexes, athletic ability, and strategy. It is commonly played 1v1 (singles) or 2v2 (doubles). While there is a lot of technique involved for intermediate to advanced play, tennis is still easy to pick up for new players and exciting at all levels of play.
