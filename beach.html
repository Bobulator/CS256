Going to the Beach

Equipment: Swimming suit (optional), towel (optional).
Weather: Anytime except during extreme wheather conditions.

The Experience
Going to the beach is a great way to enjoy the Earth's beauty and majesty. There are many activities that can be done at the beach, including swimming, surfing, beach volleyball, flying kites, and many others. Some people simply enjoy visiting the beach for the views/smells/sounds/wildlife of the ocean.
